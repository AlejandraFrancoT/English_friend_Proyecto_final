Present perfect vs simple past

Grammar focus

Use the present perfect for an indefinite time in the past. Use the simple past for a specific event in the past.
Have you ever eaten Cuban food? Yes, I have, I´ve had it many times I ate a lot of Cuban food when I lived in Miami
No, I haven´t. I haven´t tried it yet. No, I never tried it when I lived in Miami
Have you ever seen an alligator? Yes, I have. I have. I´ve seen a few alligators in my life I saw a big alligator at the new park last week.
No, I haven´t. I´ve never seen one. sI didn´t go to the alligator park last week, so I didn´t see any
