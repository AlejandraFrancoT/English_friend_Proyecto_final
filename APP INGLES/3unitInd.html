Indirect requests

Grammar focus

Statements Indirect request introduced by that
Mary, you left ypur phone in my car. Could you tell Mary(that) she left her phone in my car?
Imperatives Indirect request using infinitives
Mary don´t forget the students reports Can you tell Mary not to forget the students´reports
Yes/No questions Indirect request introduced by if or whether
Ms. Martin, have you grades our tesis? Can you ask her whether or not she is coming to the meeting?
Mary, are you coming to the meeting? Could you ask her whether or not she is coming to the meeting?
Wh-questions Indirect requests introduced by a question word
Mary, where are you having lunch? can you ask Mary she´s having lunch?
Ms.Martin, what time can I talk to you about my homework? Would you ask her what time I can talk to her about my homework?
