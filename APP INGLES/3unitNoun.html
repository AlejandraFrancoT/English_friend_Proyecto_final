Noun phrases containing relative clauses

Grammar focus

Something (that) I´d be worried about is the local food The local food is something (that) I´d be worried about.
One thing (that) I´d really miss is hanging out with my friends. Hanging out with my friends is one thing (that) I´d really miss
The people (who/that) I´d miss the most are my parents. My parents are the people (who/that) I´d miss the most
